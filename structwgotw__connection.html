Detailed Description

Encapsulates a connection recorded by the WGOtW session.

wgotw_connection keeps track of the directory that socket buffers saved into, the address (IPv4 or IPv6) of the remote host, the connection's port, and a tail pointer to the rest of the connections in the session.
